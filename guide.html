Real-Time Metrics

Current Price

Live price from the selected market, updated every 1-3 seconds.

Velocity

What it means: Rate of price change ($/second)

  • Positive (Green): Price increasing
  • Negative (Red): Price decreasing
  • Formula: (Price₂ - Price₁) / (Time₂ - Time₁)

Acceleration

What it means: Rate of velocity change ($/second²)

  • Positive (Green): Price momentum increasing
  • Negative (Red): Price momentum decreasing
  • Formula: (Velocity₂ - Velocity₁) / (Time₂ - Time₁)

Volatility Compression

What it means: Market volatility state

  • < 1.0 (Green): Volatility compressing (calming down)
  • > 1.0 (Red): Volatility expanding (getting more volatile)
  • = 1.0: Stable volatility

Mathematical Engine

Physics-Based Approach

Delta25 uses classical physics equations to predict price movements:

  • Position: Current price
  • Velocity: Rate of price change
  • Acceleration: Rate of velocity change
  • Projection Formula: P(t+Δt) = P(t) + v×Δt + ½×a×Δt²

Data Requirements

  • Rolling Window: Last 30 data points used
  • Update Frequency: Every 1-3 seconds

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • "Connection Failed": Check internet connection, try different symbol
  • "No Data Received": Symbol might not exist or have low volume
  • "Invalid Symbol": Use correct format (BTCUSDT for Binance)
  • Metrics Show "--": Need 5+ data points, wait longer

Performance Tips

  • Use Popular Pairs: BTCUSDT, ETHUSDT have best data quality
  • Avoid Low Volume: Obscure tokens may have poor predictions
  • Check Confidence: >70% confidence recommended for trading decisions
  • Monitor Volatility: High volatility = less predictable
